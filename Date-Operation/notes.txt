Common Date/Time Format Codes:
%Y: Year with century (e.g., 2024)
%m: Month as a zero-padded decimal (e.g., 10 for October)
%d: Day of the month as a zero-padded decimal (e.g., 05)
%H: Hour (24-hour clock) as a zero-padded decimal
%M: Minute as a zero-padded decimal
%S: Second as a zero-padded decimal
